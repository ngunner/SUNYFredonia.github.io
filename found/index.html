What's with the crane?

Fredonians tend to see things differently...

Take this piece of paper. Sure, it's flat, square, and brightly colored -- but we know it can be more. With the right mix of patience, instruction, science, creativity, and fun, it can be transformed into something more. Something unique. Something beautiful.

This crane symbolizes the transformation which Fredonia students experience. At Fredonia, you're encouraged to embrace your creativity -- starting right now! Make your origami "FRED" crane come to life today -- and then share it on social media from your home town or a favorite place of yours. It's a great way to let today's Fredonians know where next year's students will be coming from!
